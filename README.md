## What is Asana?

[Asana](https://asana.com/) is a work management platform for planning projects, assigning tasks, tracking goals, automating workflows, and coordinating teams across departments, founded in 2008 by Dustin Moskovitz and Justin Rosenstein. The founders previously worked at Facebook and built Asana around the idea of giving teams a shared picture of who is doing what, by when, how, and why. The platform handles task management, project views, timelines, automations, portfolios, goals, workload planning, approvals, reporting, integrations, AI Studio, and enterprise governance — the stack you would otherwise assemble from several separate tools.
